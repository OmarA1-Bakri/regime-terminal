# RESEARCH CHECKLIST

Before any trade, Claude must check ALL of the following.
Skipping a check is not allowed. Log findings for each.

## 1. Regime State (Required)

- What is the 4h regime for this symbol?
- What is the 1h regime? Do they agree?
- What was the regime 24h ago? Is this a transition or stable state?
- What does the transition matrix say about where this regime goes next?

## 2. Price Action (Required)

- Current price vs 24h high/low
- Current price vs 7d high/low
- Distance from recent support/resistance
- Volume trend (increasing or decreasing)

## 3. BTC Context (Required)

- What regime is BTC in? (If BTC is in Crash, dont go long on anything)
- BTC dominance trend (rising = risk-off, falling = alt season)

## 4. Catalyst Check (Required for High Conviction)

- Any upcoming events for this asset? (upgrades, launches, partnerships)
- Any negative catalysts? (hacks, delistings, regulatory)
- For TAO: any subnet launches or Bittensor upgrades?

## 5. Funding Rates (Optional, for future use)

- Current funding rate on perps
- If very positive: crowded long, risk of squeeze
- If very negative: crowded short, potential short squeeze

## 6. Correlation Check (Required)

- How correlated is this symbol with existing positions?
- If adding BTC-correlated asset and already hold BTC: count as same exposure
- Correlation groups: {BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB}, {TAO, FET, RENDER, NEAR}, {DOGE, XRP, ADA}

## 7. Thesis Formation (Required)

Write a 2-3 sentence thesis:
- WHY this trade (what signal, what catalyst)
- WHAT is the expected outcome (target price or regime)
- WHEN to exit (stop loss, take profit, or regime change)

Example: TAO 4h regime flipped from Bear to Neutral. 1h confirms at Weak Bull.
SN64 subnet activity spiked 40% this week suggesting growing adoption.
Enter 50% position at market, stop at -3%, target is regime flip to Bull.

## 8. Anti-Checklist (Required)

Before confirming, ask yourself:
- What could go wrong with this trade?
- Am I chasing price or reacting to a genuine signal?
- Would I take this trade if I had already lost money today?
- Is this FOMO or conviction?

If any answer raises doubt, DO NOT ENTER. Holding cash is always an option.
